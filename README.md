## About Me

I'm a **Flutter Developer at Cybrosys Technologies**, Kozhikode, Kerala — building production-grade **enterprise mobile applications** for Android and iOS, integrated with **Odoo ERP**.

With close to **2 years of hands-on experience**, I work on real-world business apps used by internal teams and external clients — covering modules like Sales, Billing, POS, Kiosk, Attendance, Expenses, and Inventory.

- Currently at **Cybrosys Technologies** contributing to the [Mobo Open Source](https://github.com/mobo-open-source) suite of Odoo mobile apps
- Full app lifecycle ownership — development → testing → deployment on **Play Store & App Store**
- Experienced in **mobile-ERP integrations**, payment workflows, and WebView-based Odoo modules
- Implemented **Dojo payment device integration** for Odoo POS
- Write **test cases** and maintain production-level codebases
- Work with **Figma** for UI design handoff and pixel-perfect implementation
- Follow **MVVM architecture** with clean, scalable code
